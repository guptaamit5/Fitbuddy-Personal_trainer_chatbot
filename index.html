🔥 Best Workout Strategy for Muscle Growth

Muscle building becomes easy if you follow the right fundamentals:

  • ✔ Train each muscle twice a week
  • ✔ Use progressive overload (increase weights or reps weekly)
  • ✔ Eat 1.6–2.2 g protein per kg of bodyweight
  • ✔ Maintain a slight calorie surplus
  • ✔ Sleep 7–9 hours daily
