Best Workout Strategy for Muscle Growth

Muscle building becomes easier when you focus on consistency, progressive overload, recovery, and proper nutrition.

  • ✔ Train each muscle group twice weekly
  • ✔ Increase weights or reps gradually
  • ✔ Eat high-protein balanced meals
  • ✔ Sleep 7–9 hours daily
  • ✔ Stay consistent for long-term progress
